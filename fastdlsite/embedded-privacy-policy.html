Privacy policy and Cookie policy things (last updated 2026-01-31) (history)

  • Information about fastdl.me:
    • fastdl.me is, and has been, operated by the same sole person since its creation, and any ownership changes and/or additional operators would be announced.
    • Servers are currently hosted in the United States of America.
  • Things collected and retained by fastdl.me:
    • Requests (IP address, HTTP request headers (e.g. referer, user-agent), geo-located country from Cloudflare's CF-IPCountry, filename, timestamp, response status) are logged for up to 72 hours (although, the logs are usually wiped daily).
      • These are collected to prevent/mitigate attacks and to debug/operate the service.
    • View-count numbers on a few pages, via the hc.fastdl.me domain (which are saved to be rendered as an image).
    • Emails sent to fastdl.me are saved until I remember to empty my inbox, which happens every month or two. Sender names/addresses are not shared, unless requested (e.g. in a map submission note).
    • Form messages sent to check.fastdl.me are deleted after map submissions have been uploaded or after a reply has been posted.
  • fastdl.me uses the services of Cloudflare, which records things separately from fastdl.me's servers, such as: site metrics (e.g. number of requests/visitors, bandwidth usage), request logs (e.g. IP addresses and other HTTP headers), and more, mostly to operate their services.
    • More information on their policies: here and here.
  • Cookies:
    • fastdl.me does not use or set cookies itself.
    • fastdl.me uses the services of Cloudflare, which might set necessary cookies (e.g. if the website is being attacked).
      More information on their policies: here and here.
  • Any questions can be sent to hello@fastdl.me
